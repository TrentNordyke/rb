16-wire Interleaved PAM-3

PAM-3 ( 3-hot encoding using 16 wires)
3 high + 10 neutral + 3 low

Different signals. The signal levels are compared with all other signals (15 other signals) and no VREF voltage is used to recover the signal. When a high signal transitions to neutral level, at the same time a neutral signal will transition to the high level.
